Lore

Rantarctica... The most prized of all the realms in Lefluatan. A place of magic, warriors and riches beyond imagining! Whoever rules Rantarctica, rules the whole Lefluatanian continent.

For thousands of years, a constant battle has raged for dominion over the realm, with creatures from all races pitting their skills against the best Rantarctica has to offer. Only the strongest can rule, and there can only ever be one. No single ruler has ever managed to reign for any length of time, but that might be about to change...

A new commander has emerged in Rantarctica. An outsider, able to unite those who were previously locked in continuous mortal combat. Are things about to change? Will you be the Battle Lord to rule them all? Will you rule the Realm of Rantarctica?

Tutorial

1. Select Difficulty

Increase the difficulty by decreasing the amount of time per round.

  • EASY: 8 seconds
  • MEDIUM: 4 seconds
  • HARD: 2 seconds

2. Select a BattleGround

A battleground can be selected by simply clicking/tapping on the desired battleground image. See all available battlegrounds by scrolling down on the screen. Clicking/tapping on Random Level will load a random battleground.

3. Battle Screen

The following can be seen on the Battle screen:

  1. Player's character image
  2. Timer
  3. AI's character image
  4. Battle info
  5. Player's character name and attributes
  6. AI's character name and attributes
  7. Card Counts

All characters have attributes, such as HIT POINTS, ATTACK, ARMOUR etc. Attribute values range from 0 to 100 (higher is better). Player attribute values are displayed in white, while AI attribute values are light blue.


At the start of each round, AI attribute values are hidden and displayed as a question mark (?). The player has to decide which of their character's attributes they believe to be high enough to beat the AI character's attribute value.

Keep an eye on the timer and ensure time doesn't run out before choosing an attribute. Note that the AI's character and name are hidden until the player selects an attribute for their character with which to battle.

4. Battle Screen: Play a Round

  1. Click/tap selected attribute
  2. AI attributes get displayed
  3. AI's character name gets displayed
  4. AI's character image gets displayed
  5. Win or Lose message

Once an attribute has been selected and clicked/tapped (highlighted in red), the AI character's attribute values, image and name will be displayed, along with a WIN or LOSE message. If the player wins, they get the AI's card which is added to their deck and vice versa.

Both the Player and AI start with 10 cards in their decks. The first to reduce the other deck's card count to 0 WINS the game!
